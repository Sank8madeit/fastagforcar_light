What is FASTag

Electronic Toll
Collection via RFID

FASTag uses Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology — a reloadable tag affixed to your vehicle's windshield that automatically deducts toll fees from a prepaid account, enabling seamless passage through toll plazas without stopping.

⚠️ Mandatory for All Vehicles

FASTag is compulsory for all four-wheeled vehicles in India since February 16, 2021. Vehicles without FASTag face double toll charges.
